Education is our future

Education

The future of any society rests on the shoulders of the young children and youths. If they are not trained to the right standard, we’ll have a society that can’t compete on the global scale. Education has a huge role to play in this. Some of the things among many my government will do to make sure our children are trained right and well to compete on the global scale are; update the curriculum from the primary level to the tertiary level, channel resources to school infrastructures and make sure thousands of student are in schools instead of the streets. Children are the future leaders of tomorrow, let’s prepare them for that.

youth Empowerment

Unemployment/Empowerment

The unemployment rate has increased over the last quarter. The statistics are hard to look at. Clear truth is the fact that there are not enough jobs to round. To solve this, we won’t just create more jobs but also give opportunities for the people to learn vocational skills. This is important because many people are don’t have the necessary skills to get a job. While jobs are created, people get empowered by learning important skills to help get employment, getting access to grants so they can start their businesses, thereby, creating more jobs and employing others.

Power for all

Electricity

Electricity is one of the basic need the government should make available for its citizens. Everyone needs light to use in their homes and businesses. For this reason, this aspect will be looked at thoroughly.

World Class Health Services

Healthcare

It is said that health is wealth. From this statement, I’ll like to say that “A healthy society is a wealthy society.” Good and affordable healthcare will be made available. Citizens deserve good healthcare especially our people in the rural areas. It will be my duty to make sure everyone has access to good healthcare and not just good, but affordable healthcare.

Free and Fair Governance

Democracy

In a democratic nation as Nigeria, it’s all about serving the people because they put us there to lead them and not rule them. My government is all about the people. Everyone should be given a chance to air their views on matters that concerns them without fear of being told to shut up. Everyone matters, without the people there would be no leader. It’s on this that true democracy rests, a government of the people, for the people, by the people.

Youths are the future

Youth Involvement

The life of any community is the youth, they are seen as the engine room of the community. The ability to carry the youth along and giving them opportunities to contribute to the development of the state will ensure a very lively state.
